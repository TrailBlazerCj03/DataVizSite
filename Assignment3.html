I would like to compare the differences between residential FAR and Commercial FAR and I select 15 different Manhattan neighborhoods. After showing the data in horizontal bars, vertical bars and radar diagram, I find that the selected neighborhoods' ratio (Residential FAR / Commercial FAR) mostly located between 1 and 100 except MN04. Through the comparison, I suspect that the neighborhood MN04 locates in Uppertown,as it has fewer commercial space and storefronts.
On the other hand, from these data, we can learn that Manhattan is really a commercial place as most of ratio is less than 10.
