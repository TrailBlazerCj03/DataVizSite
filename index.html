Hello, I am Jiacheng Zhou. I come from China and I'm now a graduate student (first year) from GSAPP (Graduate School of Architecture, Planning and Preservation) in Columbia University and my major is Urban Planning.
My interests are now concentrated on urban data mining, analytics and visualisation. Harnessing the power of information technology to support an integrated, transdisciplinary planning approach that engages the large scale and complexity of future city systems is the new trend for urban planninng. I hope I can use these new technological advances to help citizens, communities and authorities understand the functioning of cities and the consequences of different planning options.
